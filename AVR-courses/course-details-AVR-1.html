Augmented & VR: Future of Interactive Worlds

$89.00 FREE

Objective

The Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) course aims to provide a thorough understanding of immersive technologies and their applications. Key objectives include learning the fundamentals of AR and VR technologies, including hardware and software components. Students will explore the development and design of AR and VR experiences, focusing on user interaction and interface design. The course also covers practical applications in various fields such as gaming, education, and simulation. Additionally, it emphasizes the challenges and future trends in AR and VR, equipping students with the skills to create and evaluate immersive experiences.

Course Outline

Overview of AR and VR technologies.Differences between AR, VR, and Mixed Reality (MR).Hardware and software components.
The Principles of user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design for AR/VR.Interaction techniques and user engagement strategies 3D modeling and spatial design.
Introduction to AR/VR development platforms (e.g., Unity, Unreal Engine). Creating AR/VR applications: workflows and best practices .Scripting and programming for AR/VR environments.
Real-world use cases in gaming, education, healthcare, and more .In Case studies of successful AR/VR implementations.Exploring the potential and limitations of AR/VR in various fields.
Addressing technical challenges: latency, resolution, and hardware limitations.Ethical considerations in AR/VR development.Emerging trends and the future of immersive technologies.
