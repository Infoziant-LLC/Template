Neural Networks and Deep Learning Course

$89.00 FREE

Objective

In this course, you'll explore the fascinating world of neural networks, the backbone of modern deep learning. You'll understand how these networks mimic the human brain to process data, recognize patterns, and make decisions. Through hands-on projects, you will build and train deep learning models that can perform complex tasks such as image recognition, natural language processing, and more. By the end of this course, you'll gain practical experience and a solid theoretical foundation, equipping you to tackle real-world challenges in AI.

Course Outline

Explore the fundamentals of neural networks, including neurons, activation functions, and architectures. Learn how networks are trained using backpropagation and gradient descent.
Dive into advanced architectures such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for image processing and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) for sequential data. Understand their unique structures and applications.
Master techniques for training deep networks, including optimization algorithms, regularization methods, and strategies to avoid overfitting. Gain insights into hyperparameter tuning and model evaluation.
Apply your knowledge to real-world problems, including image classification, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning. Work on projects that demonstrate the power of deep learning.
Examine the ethical implications of AI and deep learning. Discuss topics such as bias, fairness, and the societal impact of deploying AI systems in various domains.
