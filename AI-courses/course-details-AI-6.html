Build AI Apps with ChatGPT, DALL-E, and GPT-4

$89.00 FREE

Objective

In the "Build AI Apps with ChatGPT, DALL-E, and GPT-4" course, participants will learn to harness the power of cutting-edge AI models for creating innovative applications. They will gain hands-on experience with ChatGPT for conversational AI, DALL-E for image generation, and GPT-4 for advanced text processing. The course emphasizes practical skills, guiding learners to integrate these models into real-world projects. By the end, participants will be equipped to develop AI-powered apps that enhance user experiences across various domains.

Course Outline

This module provides an overview of the key AI models, explaining their functionalities, capabilities, and the differences between them. Learners will explore the foundational concepts of conversational AI, image generation, and text processing, setting the stage for deeper dives into each model.
In this module, participants will learn how to create interactive chatbots using ChatGPT. The focus will be on designing effective conversational flows, handling user input, and integrating the chatbot into various platforms. Practical exercises will ensure learners can build and deploy their own ChatGPT-powered apps.
This section introduces learners to DALL-E, teaching them how to generate unique images from textual descriptions. Participants will explore the creative possibilities of DALL-E, learning to craft prompts that yield desired visual outputs. Real-world applications, like marketing and content creation, will be covered.
Learners will dive deep into the capabilities of GPT-4 for text generation, summarization, translation, and more. This module covers best practices for prompt engineering, fine-tuning model outputs, and integrating GPT-4 into diverse applications. Participants will practice creating AI-driven text solutions tailored to specific needs.
The final module focuses on bringing everything together by embedding ChatGPT, DALL-E, and GPT-4 into comprehensive AI applications. Learners will explore how to build user interfaces, manage data flow, and deploy AI-powered apps. By the end, they will have the skills to create robust, AI-enhanced products.
